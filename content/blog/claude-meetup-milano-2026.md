## Loop engineering: from prompts to loops

**Alexio Cassani** organised the event and set the tone with an opening talk on the next direction for agentic work: **loop engineering**. The core idea: you no longer write one prompt at a time; you design loops that orchestrate your agents. Arrange it once, hit go, and let the loop do the prompting. In my view, it's here to stay.

## Two talks I'm taking home

Among the many things I'm taking home from the evening, two stand out.

**Andrea Di Lisio** (Sr. SWE, TrueLayer) showed how **Claude Code skills** can become a tool to document and share team knowledge instead of leaving hard-won know-how stuck in individual heads.

**Pasquale Morelli** (Sr. Brand Designer, TrueLayer) shared how AI and Claude Code are changing brand design processes: from their use of Weavy, now [Figma Weave](https://weave.figma.com/) (wow!), to prototyping and building AI systems, with designers shipping changes without the usual ticket loop.

## Takeaways

Seeing all these ideas implemented live in a regulated environment like fintech was super interesting. The common thread across every talk: stop being the bottleneck, design the loop, and let your agents run.
